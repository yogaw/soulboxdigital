SOULBOX PLATFORM

From Vision to Phase 1 Execution

January 2026

Participants: Owner, Ops, CS, Coach Rep

Workshop ini bertujuan menyamakan visi Soulbox ke depan, mengidentifikasi pain point utama operasional hari ini, dan menyepakati fokus Phase 1 platform agar operasional lebih rapi, tenang, dan scalable.

02
Phased Platform Roadmap
Perjalanan menuju platform yang scalable
Phase 1
RAPIH
Operasional tenang. Booking jelas, data tidak tercecer, aturan main tegas.
↓
Phase 2
TERUKUR
Keputusan berbasis data. Semua aktivitas terbaca, owner & manager tahu apa yang terjadi.
↓
Phase 3
PINTAR
Personalisasi & optimasi. Sistem bantu ambil keputusan, antisipatif bukan reaktif.
↓
Phase 4
BERDAYA
Ecosystem & scale. Value lebih tinggi untuk brand & community, siap kolaborasi.

Kenapa Bertahap? Mem-build platform sekaligus berisiko tinggi. Dengan pendekatan phased, kita bisa fokus pada stabilitas dulu, belajar dari realita, dan mengurangi risk of failure.

03
Phase 1 – Agreed Scope
Fokus: Operasional rapi, tenang, scalable
A
Booking & Schedule
  • Booking & slot real-time
  • Aturan cancel & waiting list jelas
  • Reminder otomatis
  • CS hanya handle exception
B
Member & Basic Data
  • Profil & status paket jelas
  • Eligibility booking otomatis
  • Riwayat kelas basic
  • Satu sumber data bersama
C
Coach & Class Visibility
  • Jadwal & kapasitas jelas
  • Coach assignment transparan
  • Status perubahan tercatat
  • Semua lihat info sama
D
Payment & Basic Tracking
  • Status payment & paket jelas
  • Booking terhubung status paket
  • Reminder masa berlaku
  • Bukan accounting system
04
What's In vs What's Out
Menjaga scope tetap fokus
Included in Phase 1
  • Booking & ops stabilityReal-time booking, clear rules, automated reminders
  • Basic visibility & reportingMember profile, package status, class history
  • Clear rules & boundariesCancel policy, no-show rules, waiting list
  • Single source of truthSatu sistem untuk semua data
Next Phases
  • Advanced analyticsDeep insights, predictive analytics, cohort analysis
  • AI / automationSmart scheduling, personalized recommendations
  • Performance scoringCoach KPI tracking, member progress
  • Complex finance featuresFull accounting, auto-reconciliation

Kenapa boundary penting? Phase 1 fokus pada stabilitas operasional. Fitur lanjutan akan ditambahkan berdasarkan learnings dari Phase 1.
